# GUTTS Company Context

This file captures a working understanding of GUTTS so future automation, content, and product work can use shared context without re-explaining the business each time.

## Company Overview

GUTTS stands for Ground Up Trade & Talent Solutions.

GUTTS is a Fairfax, Virginia-based workforce development company focused on creating career pathways into the skilled trades, especially Plumbing and HVAC. The company combines accredited entry-level training with direct job placement support so that motivated individuals can move into stable, living-wage careers and employers can hire dependable, job-ready entry-level talent.

Website: https://www.gutts.tech/

## Core Business Model

GUTTS operates across two connected functions:

1. Training
   - Short-term, accredited, entry-level Plumbing and HVAC programs
   - Hands-on technical instruction plus soft-skills development
   - Certifications and credential value tied to career progression

2. Job Placement
   - Resume support
   - Interview preparation
   - Interview coordination
   - Direct employer connections
   - Placement into entry-level trade roles

The website also explains that:

- GUTTS LLC handles employer engagement, workforce services, and job placement
- The GUTTS Opportunity and Impact Fund expands equitable access to subsidized and tuition-free training through a nonprofit structure

## Main Value Proposition

GUTTS is not only a training provider. It is a career-launch and talent-pipeline platform.

For students:
- Learn real, practical trade skills quickly
- Earn recognized credentials
- Get help landing a first job in the trades

For employers:
- Access pre-trained, vetted, entry-level trade talent
- Reduce onboarding burden
- Lower recruiting cost and hiring risk
